Fx Augment Demo - Audio Comparison

This demo showcases the progression of audio stems through different processing stages:

  • ORIGINAL_STEMS - Original audio stems, processed from engineer
  • DRY_STEMS - Linear Sum of RAWs, unprocessed audio stems
  • DRY_STEMS + NORM - Normalized audio stems
  • DRY_STEMS + NORM + AUGMENT - Normalized and augmented stems with effects processing

About This Demo

This demonstration showcases the audio processing pipeline for the track "Back in Black" by AC/DC. Each row represents a different stem (isolated instrument or vocal track), and you can compare how the audio changes through the processing stages.

Note: Your browser must support FLAC audio playback for the audio players to work properly. Most modern browsers support FLAC natively.
